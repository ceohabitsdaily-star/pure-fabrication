What Is Aluminum Panel Cladding

The material that defines modern buildings.

Aluminum panel cladding — commonly known as ACM (Aluminum Composite Material) — is an exterior wall covering system made from two thin aluminum sheets bonded to a solid core. The result is a panel that is lightweight, rigid, weather-resistant, and available in virtually any colour or finish.

It is the material behind most modern building facades you see in Toronto today: the clean flat surfaces, the sharp reveals between panels, the matte charcoal or brushed silver finishes on custom homes and commercial buildings. If a building looks architectural, there is a good chance ACM is involved.

Beyond aesthetics, aluminum cladding protects the building envelope from moisture, wind, and temperature changes — making it one of the most practical and durable exterior systems available for Canadian climates.

Lightweight & Strong

ACM panels weigh a fraction of solid metal or stone, reducing structural load while maintaining rigidity across large facade surfaces.

Weather & UV Resistant

Designed for harsh Canadian climates — freeze-thaw cycles, heavy rain, UV exposure. The finish holds colour and gloss for decades without fading.

Fully Customizable

Flat, perforated, brushed, matte, gloss, wood-grain, stone-effect — panels can be fabricated in nearly any size, shape, and finish.

Low Maintenance

Once installed, aluminum panels require minimal upkeep. No painting, no sealing. A periodic wash keeps the facade looking new for 20–30 years.

Types of Panels

What we fabricate.

01

ACM / ACP Panels

The most widely used exterior cladding system. Two aluminum skins bonded to a polyethylene or fire-resistant core. Available in matte, gloss, metallic, brushed, and custom finishes. Used on residential facades, commercial buildings, and high-rises.

02

Solid Aluminum Plate

Thicker, heavier, and more architecturally assertive than composite panels. Used when the design calls for a stronger material presence — feature walls, canopies, soffits, and bold accent elements. Non-combustible and extremely durable.

03

Custom Fabricated Panels

Not every facade fits a standard panel. We fabricate custom shapes, angles, returns, corners, and reveals to match any architectural drawing. Complex geometry, unusual dimensions, or site-specific conditions — we cut and form it to spec.

Why Aluminum Cladding

The reasons architects
and builders choose it.

Building Envelope Protection

A properly installed cladding system acts as a rainscreen — keeping water, wind, and cold away from the building structure. This protects the wall assembly, prevents moisture damage, and improves insulation performance.

Design Flexibility

Aluminum cladding is one of the few exterior systems that gives architects true freedom — precise panel sizing, flush joints, deep reveals, colour-matched trims. The result is a facade that looks intentional, not assembled.

Long-Term Value

Compared to brick, stone, or wood siding, aluminum panels hold their appearance and structural integrity far longer with far less maintenance. For both residential and commercial properties, cladding adds lasting curb value.

Residential & Commercial

Aluminum cladding works across project types — custom homes, infill builds, mid-rise developments, retail facades, office buildings. The system scales from a single feature wall to a full multi-storey envelope.
