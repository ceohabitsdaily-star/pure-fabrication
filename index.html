All Services
01

Aluminum Panel Cladding

Our core specialty. We supply and install aluminum composite panels (ACM) for residential and commercial facades. Matte, gloss, brushed, and custom finishes. Panels are precision-cut, folded, and installed with concealed fixing systems for a seamless, flush result.

02

Facade Systems & Design

Full facade envelope design and installation — from substructure and thermal breaks to the final panel face. We work directly with architects, general contractors, and developers from shop drawings through site completion.

03

Custom Fabrication

Bespoke panel shapes, reveal joints, corner details, and feature cladding elements. If it involves aluminum, we fabricate it in-house — signage panels, canopies, soffits, column wraps, and architectural accent elements.

How We Work

Precision at
every stage.

01

Site Assessment

We visit the site before anything else. Measurements, substrate conditions, wind load requirements, and drainage details are all documented before a single panel is specified.

02

Shop Drawings & Approval

We produce detailed fabrication and installation drawings for every project — panel layout, reveal joints, corner conditions, substructure layout. Nothing is cut until drawings are approved.

03

Panel Fabrication

Panels are precision-cut and folded in our facility using CNC routing and hydraulic press brakes. Every corner, return, and reveal is fabricated to exact tolerance before leaving the shop.

04

Installation & Handover

Our installation crews handle the complete envelope — substructure, thermal spacers, panel fixing, sealants, and final inspection. We don't leave until the facade is perfect.
