What We Do

Five specialties.
One standard.

From raw panel to finished facade — we handle every stage in-house. Supply, design, fabrication, and installation under one roof, with one standard of precision throughout.

01

Aluminum Panel
Cladding

Our core specialty. We supply and install aluminum composite panels (ACM/ACP) for residential and commercial facades across Toronto and the GTA. Every panel is precision-cut, folded, and installed with concealed fixing systems for a seamless, flush result in matte, gloss, brushed, and custom finishes.

  • Residential & commercial facades
  • ACM / ACP panel systems
  • Concealed fixing & reveal joints
  • Matte, gloss, brushed & custom finishes
  • OBC-compliant FR-rated cores
02

Facade Systems
& Design

Full facade envelope design and installation — from substructure and thermal breaks to the final panel face. We work directly with architects, general contractors, and developers from shop drawings through site completion, ensuring every condition is resolved before fabrication begins.

  • Full envelope design & coordination
  • Substructure & thermal break systems
  • Shop drawings & panel layout
  • Corner, soffit & reveal detailing
  • Site-verified measurements
03

Custom
Fabrication

Bespoke panel shapes, reveal joints, corner details, and feature cladding elements fabricated in-house using CNC routing and hydraulic press brakes. Every corner, return, and reveal is cut to exact tolerance. If it involves aluminum, we fabricate it — signage panels, canopies, soffits, column wraps, and architectural accent elements.

  • CNC routing & hydraulic press brake
  • ±0.5mm fabrication tolerance
  • Canopies, soffits & column wraps
  • Signage panels & accent elements
  • Complex geometry & curved panels
04

Design
Assist

We engage early — before fabrication — to coordinate facade geometry, resolve joint conditions, and translate architectural intent into buildable shop drawings. Working alongside architects and GCs during design development prevents costly changes on site and delivers facades that read exactly as intended.

  • Early-stage facade coordination
  • Architectural shop drawings
  • Panel layout & joint resolution
  • 3D facade layout preview
  • Specification & finish guidance
05

Material Supply

Need panels fabricated and delivered without full installation? We start from raw aluminum sheet, cut and form every panel in-house to your exact specifications, and supply them ready to install. Whether you're an architect specifying a finish, a builder sourcing panels, or a contractor who has their own crew on site — we fabricate it, you install it.

  • Fabricated from raw aluminum sheet
  • Cut-to-size, folded & finished in-house
  • Any colour, finish, or panel size
  • GTA & Ontario-wide delivery
  • Architect & contractor accounts

Standards & Compliance

Built to code.
Built to last.

Ontario Building Code (OBC) Compliance

All facade systems are engineered to meet OBC requirements. We specify FR-rated (Fire-Retardant) cores and materials tested to ASTM E84 and CAN/ULC-S134 standards for non-combustible construction.

±0.5mm Fabrication Tolerance

In-house CNC fabrication maintains sub-millimeter precision on all panel folds and cuts. Mitered corners and complex transitions align perfectly with site-verified layouts — every time.

Site-Verified Layouts

We visit the site before anything is cut. Measurements, substrate conditions, wind load requirements, and drainage details are documented and confirmed before a single panel is specified.
